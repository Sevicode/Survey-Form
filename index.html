How Do You Commute to Work? A Survey

The Growth in our population has created a big challange for people who need to commute to work everyday. Every hour staying in traffic is the time we loose to be more productive or spend with our family. For this reason, the goal is to encourage working population in big cities to use public transport more frenquently when commuting to work. The following survey is created to collect statistcial data on the percentage of population who uses public transport when commuting to work. The answers to the following questions will assist us on creating a better public transport system for our Great Brisbane Area.
